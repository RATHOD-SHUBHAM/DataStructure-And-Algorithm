Approach for Inserting in Doubly Linked List.

There are 5 approaches for Inserting in doubly linked list:

# ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1] Insert in the front.

# ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

2] Insert at the end.

# ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

3] Insert After a given node.

# ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

4] Insert Before a given node.

# ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

5] Insert at a position.

# ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
